[ Installation ]

Requirements: mIRC 6.XX

1. Extract files to <mIRC dir>\mIRCDLL\
2. Open mIRCDLL.vbp with Visual Basic 6
3. Compile mIRCDLL.DLL to <mirc dir>\mIRCDLL\
4. From mIRC, type /load -rs mIRCDLL\mIRCDLL.mrc
5. From mIRC, Type /mIRCDLL
